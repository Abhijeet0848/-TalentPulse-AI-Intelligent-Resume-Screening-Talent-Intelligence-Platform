Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TalentPulse AI

Does it support PDF, DOCX, and TXT resume files?
Yes! TalentPulse AI includes a multi-format parsing engine built on PyPDF2, pdfplumber, and python-docx to extract text, contact info, degrees, and technical skills seamlessly across all major document formats.
How does the AI candidate match scoring algorithm work?
Our match score utilizes a multi-factor weighted matrix: 40% TF-IDF Cosine Vector Similarity + 30% Skill Coverage Match + 20% Experience Timeline Alignment + 10% Required Education Alignment.
Can multiple recruiters collaborate with different access roles?
Absolutely. TalentPulse AI features Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) supporting 3 distinct roles: Technical Recruiter, HR Manager, and Administrator, each with tailored navigation menus and permissions.
Is my candidate resume data kept private and secure?
Yes. All candidate records are stored in an encrypted database with password hashing and session authorization. Data is never shared or used to train public external LLMs.
